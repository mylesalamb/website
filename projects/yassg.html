YASSG (Yet Another Static Site Generator), is a tool that I wrote to generate my website, as I was unhappy with other offerings that already existed (Pelican, Jekyll).

Whilst other SSGs are featureful, all I really needed or preferred was a simple abstraction over Jinja2, that I could embelish with features as and when I required them.

Since 2021 I have been sporadically adding features and improvements, as and when they are required by this website, as such the tool is now generally in a usable state. Which I believe this website attests to.
